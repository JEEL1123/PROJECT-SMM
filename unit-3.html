Unit III – Email Marketing (Brand Promotion)

This page explains how Electronic Marketing tools such as Email and Mobile Marketing are used to promote brands, increase awareness, and build strong customer relationships.

Email Marketing Strategy for Brand Promotion

Email marketing helps brands communicate directly with customers through promotional campaigns, product announcements, brand stories, and special offers. A strong strategy includes building an email list, segmenting audiences based on interests, and sending personalized content to improve engagement and conversions.

Types of Emails Used in Brand Promotion

Promotional Emails

Used to promote new products, discounts, seasonal offers, and brand campaigns to increase visibility and sales.

Newsletter Emails

Monthly newsletters sharing brand updates, success stories, marketing tips, blog articles, and upcoming promotions.

Transactional Emails

Automated emails such as order confirmations, account updates, subscription confirmations, and service notifications.

Mobile Marketing Strategy for Brand Promotion

Mobile marketing focuses on reaching customers through smartphones using responsive websites, SMS campaigns, WhatsApp marketing, push notifications, mobile ads, and social media platforms. It ensures that brand messages reach customers anytime and anywhere.
